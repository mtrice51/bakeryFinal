Iced Latte

Rudy's Iced Lattes have a smooth, roasted blend of our imported, exotic coffee beans with creamy milk and ice to make a refreshing treat.

Cold Brew

Rudy's Cold Brew coffee is made by steeping coffee grounds in water at cool temperatures for an extended period. Our coarse-ground beans are soaked in water for about 12 to 24 hours, and the finished product is a glass of our delicous, house made Cold Brew.

Espresso

Rudy's Espresso is a thicker, concentrated form of our house blend of coffee beans. This tiny, yet powerful coffee packs a punch that will energize your day.

Macchiato

Rudy's Macchiato is an espresso coffee drink, topped with a small amount of foamed or steamed milk that allows the taste of the espresso to still shine through.
